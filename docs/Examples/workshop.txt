Total participants	        15
Group size	                3
Number of groups	        5
Study design	            crossover
Order split             	2 groups classical-first, 3 groups HALO-first
Roles per group	            interfaces, IPC/config, integration/test
Unit of comparison	        group-level
Main outputs	            time, changed files, success, survey


Participant assignment inside each group:
Person	            Best fit
Person A	        more architecture/modeling oriented
Person B	        more embedded / low-level / config oriented
Person C	        more build, debug, integration oriented

Not assigning one participant permanently to Linux, one to Zephyr, one to FreeRTOS.
That makes the study measure platform difficulty instead of method quality.

Every group works on the whole design. The platform details are already scaffolded
and the comparison stays focused on classical vs HALO
